About

I am a second-year undergraduate student in the Department of Artificial Intelligence Engineering at Istanbul University. My research interests lie in computer vision and deep learning, with a focus on supervised learning, self-supervised learning, and explainable AI (XAI). I am particularly interested in understanding how deep models learn from visual data and in developing methods to make their predictions more transparent and interpretable.
